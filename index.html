The key i opens/closes this menu.

The Arrow keys rotate the cube along the X- and Y-axes

The keys 4 and 6 rotate the cube along the Z-axis.

Be aware that the axes stay the same after every rotation.

The key t (resp. T) rotates the top layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key b (resp. B) rotates the bottom layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key l (resp. L) rotates the left layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key r (resp. R) rotates the right layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key f (resp. F) rotates the front layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key b (resp. B) rotates the back layer (anti-)clockwise.

The key u undoes the last rotation.

The key s scrambles the cube.
