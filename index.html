The key i opens/closes this menu.


The Arrow keys rotate the cube along the X- and Y-axes

The keys 4 and 6 rotate the cube along the Z-axis.

Be aware that the axes stay the same after every rotation.

The keys +/- zoom the cube in/out.


The key t (resp. T) rotates the top layer.

The key d (resp. D) rotates the down layer.

The key l (resp. L) rotates the left layer.

The key r (resp. R) rotates the right layer.

The key f (resp. F) rotates the front layer.

The key b (resp. B) rotates the back layer.

The key e (resp. E) rotates the equator layer.

The key m (resp. M) rotates the middle layer.

The key s (resp. S) rotates the standing layer.


The key u undoes the last layer rotation.

The key X scrambles the cube.

The key c toggles the transparent mode.
